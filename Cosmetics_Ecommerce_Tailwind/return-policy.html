Return & Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is important to us. Please review our return policy below.

Return Eligibility

  1. Products must be returned within 7 days of delivery.
  2. Items must be unused and in original packaging.
  3. Certain hygiene products (lipsticks, makeup applicators) are non-returnable once opened.

Refund Process

StepDescription
1Request a return from your Profile page
2Pack the item securely
3Courier partner picks up the item
4Refund credited within 5-7 business days

Exchange Policy

We offer free exchanges for size or shade mismatches within 7 days of delivery.

Need to start a return? Visit your Profile or contact support.
